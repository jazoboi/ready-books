  \item[\textbf{Whirr}]      A buzzing drone \textemdash{} sees the world from above.
  \item[\textbf{Sprocket}]   A wheeled rover \textemdash{} explores where humans cannot go.

  \item[\textbf{Sparks}]     A micro-bot surgeon \textemdash{} tiny but incredibly careful.
  \item[\textbf{Tread}]      A heavy-duty rescue robot \textemdash{} brave and unstoppable.
