Here are the friendly characters who will guide you through the secret life of the internet:

\characterparade

\begin{description}[labelwidth=3.5cm, leftmargin=3.7cm]
  \item[\textbf{Dot}] A tiny glowing packet of information — your main guide.
  \item[\textbf{Bee Browser}] A cheerful bee who takes you to any website.
  \item[\textbf{Willa Wi-Fi}] Invisible waves that carry signals through the air.
  \item[\textbf{Rory Router}] The friendly box that directs traffic in your home.
  \item[\textbf{Sunny Server}] A powerful computer that stores and sends information.
  \item[\textbf{Captain Cable}] The undersea cable that carries data across oceans.
  \item[\textbf{Scout Search}] The detective who finds anything on the internet.
  \item[\textbf{Cloud Pal}] Faraway computers that store your files safely.
\end{description}

\section*{Welcome}
\markboth{Welcome}{}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Welcome}
Welcome, explorer! In this book, we will follow tiny bits of information as they zip through homes, neighbourhoods, oceans, and giant computer buildings. We will meet friendly helpers and learn how websites, Wi-Fi, routers, servers, search engines, and the cloud all work together.
