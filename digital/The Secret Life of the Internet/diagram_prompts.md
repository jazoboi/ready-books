# Diagram Image Prompts — The Secret Life of the Internet (EPUB)

**Style Direction (apply to ALL):**
> Clean, flat, infographic-style educational diagram. Bold colours from the InternetBlue
> (#2196F3) palette. Simple labelled nodes connected by arrows. White background.
> Suitable for children ages 8–12. No complex 3D effects. High contrast for small screens.
> Minimum 1200px wide, PNG format.
