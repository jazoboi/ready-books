\begin{description}[labelwidth=3.5cm, leftmargin=3.7cm]
  \item[\textbf{Dot}]        Returning from Book 1 — a glowing packet of information, your main guide.
  \item[\textbf{Chip}]       A tiny silicon processor — the brain of the phone, always thinking.
  \item[\textbf{Memo}]       A cheerful memory card who never forgets a thing.
  \item[\textbf{Tappy}]      A lively fingertip who brings the touchscreen to life.
  \item[\textbf{Lens}]       A clever little camera lens who loves capturing moments.
  \item[\textbf{Zap}]        A buzzing battery cell, full of energy and ready to power up.
  \item[\textbf{Sensa}]      A curious sensor cluster who feels every tilt and twist.
  \item[\textbf{Appy}]       A busy worker bee who runs all your favourite apps.
  \item[\textbf{Orbi}]       A satellite orbiting high above Earth, helping phones find their way.
\end{description}

\section*{Welcome}
\markboth{Welcome}{}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Welcome}
Last time we explored the Internet — the giant network that connects the world.
Now it is time to open up the tiny computer you carry in your pocket!
We will shrink down and travel inside a smartphone, meeting the amazing parts
that work together to take photos, play music, find your location, and keep you connected.
Are you ready? Follow Dot inside!
